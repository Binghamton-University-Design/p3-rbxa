RECREATION PARK
42.10째 N, 75.93째 W
Beethoven St & Seminary Ave, Binghamton, NY
WHERE TO GO
There is a large, open space on the corner of Laurel and Seminary. It overlooks the upper baseball field and the pool. Take a seat on one of the benches, find a spot in the grass, or walk along the paved trails and watch the sun sink behind the hills.
THE BEST PART
This is a great park for families, students, and anyone who loves a well-loved, peaceful outdoor space. No need to drive here if you do not have a car, and there is plenty of space to settle in and enjoy the view.
